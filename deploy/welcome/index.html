🧳 HATPro on Archon

A working demo of the DIF Hospitality & Travel Profile built on Archon did:cid — travellers share verified, self-custodied data with suppliers, privately and selectively.

Three small apps play the three roles in a HATPro exchange. A traveller builds a profile and holds credentials in their own browser; a supplier requests and verifies them; a trust registry decides which credential issuers are trusted. No personal data is stored by the supplier.

Try the full flow

About 3 minutes, two browser tabs.

  1. Open the Traveler Wallet and create your wallet (just a name). You'll receive verified Over-18 and Loyalty credentials automatically.
  2. In the wallet's Profile tab, edit your preferences and Save — this self-issues your HATPro profile.
  3. In the Supplier Console, choose what to request and click Create request; copy the challenge DID.
  4. Back in the wallet's Requests tab, paste it → Review → Consent & present; copy the response DID.
  5. In the Supplier Console, paste the response DID and Verify — you'll see ✓ ACCEPTED, with your verified credentials and your self-asserted profile.
Demoing with others? Each person should use their own browser (or a private/incognito window) so they get their own wallet — the supplier then receives each person's unique profile.
